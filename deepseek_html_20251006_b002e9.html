The Challenge

Why KMRL needs an intelligent solution for train induction planning

Manual Reconciliation

Fleet information scattered across 6+ siloed systems including spreadsheets, WhatsApp updates, and Maximo logs requires 2+ hours of nightly manual reconciliation.

Unscheduled Withdrawals

Missing a single telecom clearance forces unscheduled rake withdrawals, directly impacting the 99.5% punctuality KPI and passenger experience.

Cost Inefficiencies

Uneven mileage assignment accelerates component fatigue while excessive shunting increases energy consumption and safety risks.

SLA Compliance Risks

Inadequate visibility into branding priorities risks breaching advertiser SLAs, exposing KMRL to significant revenue penalties.

Our Solution

How MetroMind transforms train induction planning through AI and optimization

System Architecture

MetroMind integrates data from multiple sources and applies intelligent algorithms to generate optimal induction plans.

System Architecture Diagram Would Appear Here

Intelligent Optimization

Multi-objective genetic algorithm that evaluates millions of possible combinations to find the optimal train set selection.

Machine Learning Predictions

XGBoost models predict failure risks with 92% accuracy, enabling proactive maintenance planning.

Digital Twin

3D visualization of depot layout with simulation capabilities for shunting operations and what-if analysis.

Explainable AI

Clear explanations for every decision, building trust with operations teams and enabling informed overrides.

Multilingual Chatbot

Natural language interface in Malayalam and English for quick queries and status updates.

Real-time Alerts

Mobile notifications for critical events, schedule changes, and approval workflows.
